French Press Coffee

    • Boil four to six cups of water. After boiling has occured, leave the water to sit for one minute.
    • Put three to four tablespoons of coffee into the French Press beaker.
  2. Pour the hot water into the French Press. Place the lid on top of the French Press, but do not press yet. Let the water steep for four minutes.
  3. After four minutes of steeping has passed, press the top of the lid as far down as it will go.
  4. Serve hot!
