Mac & Cheese

What better way to enjoy a delicious batch of cheese than to make macaroni with it?

  1. Boil six cups of water.
  2. Add macaroni noodles and stir for seven minutes.
  3. At end of seven minutes, drain water from macaroni.
  4. Add half a cup of cheese, four tablespoons of butter, and one quarter cup of milk.
  5. Stir to perfection and enjoy!
